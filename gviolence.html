Stop Violence against Women

Everyone has a right to a safe and healthy relationship. Many women do not take into consideration these small details that could lead them to fall into an unhealthy relationship. Women might take the feeling of control and isolation coming from her partner as something normal. Whereas it is just the start of an unhealthy relationship. Women should know that it is okay to say no. That it is not your fault. They will be able to unpack the 3 steps towards violence and end the unhealthy relationship.

The target audience for this campaign are, Adult teenagers that wishes to find price charming and live a romantic story. Ages: 16 til 23. They do not know how to dierentiate between an unhealthy and a healthy relationship. They take might take controlling and isolating as part of a normal relationship. They are strong cofident and innocent but CONFUSED. They hesitate when questioned: 'How truely soulmate is your soulmate?'

It is very important to know the best time to launch a campaign. For this campaign the best timing would be MID OF JULY School and university students are exicted to begin with summer. And what is better than summer love. They have the time to meet new prince charming to spend beautiful summer moments together. For the ones that are already in relationship, summer is the time to let them know their partners personality more.
