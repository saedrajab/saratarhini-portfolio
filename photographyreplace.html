Capture the moment

Photography can capture someone's heart and soul. Even the simplest photo has a story that tails along with it. It is an escape from the real world; a creation that narrows on one particular subject and carefully details their every move and emotion.

Landscape Photography

Landscape photography shows spaces within the world, sometimes vast and unending, but other times microscopic. Landscape photographs typically capture the presence of nature. The visual focuces on the rule of thirds composition.

Still life Photography

Still life image is a work of art that focuses on inanimate subjects. Usually, the subjects are commonplace objects. Expressing thoughts through photography is something I enjoy. I call this image : "Look out the window", the windows are eyes of the soul.

Douma, Lebanon | 06:48 pm | 10 of May, 2015

Emotion through Photography

The image was used on billboards and posters. The concept was to show the women who suffer form violence by their partner at home, however, they are not able to make any action. They are not happy, but they can not speak. The visual focus more on the eyes. Since the eyes are the window of the soul.
