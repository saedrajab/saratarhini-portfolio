Illustration

Illustration is a visual interpretation of a particular concept, text or process. It's an image that aims at supporting, clarifying or even extending the ideas. Illustration is as powerful as photography, however, it depends on the project one is working with. Illustration always helps me to go out of the box and create something abstract but meaningful, it offers full on creativity with no boundaries.

Story telling through Illustration

'Share3 Al nadam' is a project  based on a story of my creation. The illustration are made mainstakingly to capture the right emotions each character has to offer. The story is made up from three characters. A mother, daughter and the sneaky neighbor. The Character are given a Local look rather than a western look.

The story introduces more than one style design. It is a combination of Design styles, Photography,Illustration and Popart. The photographs were taken from the beautiful building found in Ashrafieh, Lebanon.

Heya Dameer Monfasel

Illustration in design is used to generate strong messages to public audience. This project was made to impower women. It holds messages that strickly implies that women can achieve success, without the help of a male partner. She can create her own space of value and privacy.

The project shows a series of Lebanese stamps under the title: 'Heya Dameer Monfasel' that clearly  implies she is capable of attaining greatness. The Illustration is based on sillhouette of a women joined with the lines of freedom. She has the full power to move these lines the way she desires.
