Writing Art

One of the most prominent steps in solving a project or creating an identity is to develop a writing research. This research will include all the key elements that will be then translated into design to call for action or provide a product/service. This section will show three different project that started with words and developed into effective design.

Ared Waqef

Azarieh street was never a business sector, that is it never had dorms. It is owned by Lebanese Batrak, who decided to make Azariah street a place of education and to help the people with disabilities and handicap. Later on an organization called “You are my brother” establishes in Azarieh, helping people with disabilities. It then changed to a small indoor school offering living for the orphans. However, they wanted to make profit. So,  They  turned this space from being inclusive and for everyone to a business project that provides indoor dorms for students and workers.

La Ween Wasel

'Laween Wasel' is an inclusive public transportation Application that serves 1st Bus Assistant platform in Lebanon. The Application gives its users accurate timing to reach their destination. Moreover, it highlights the need of increase in transportation means for handicap and disability.

Ashrafieh

This project was all about observing and taking notes. I focused a specific geographic area in Lebanon. I studied and observed the people in specifc area at different times of the day. At the end of the research I came across several key words that perfectly discribes the geographic area with its people streets, transportatin. From these key words a brand identity was developed.
