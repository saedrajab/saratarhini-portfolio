Creating an Identity

A logo brings an idea into real life. It creates its own Brand Identity and gives it unique Brand personality, that disgushes the Brand from what is found in the market. The following  LOGOS  were created and implemented in the market across the world.

The Beirut Banyan

The Banyan tree, found at AUB Campus, it is the only Banyan tree found in Lebanon. Its roots grow horizontally. Each root caries a certain story, all these stories are linked to one main topic, lebanon. The Beirut Banyan is a podcast that shares unique stories addressing sensitive topics by interviewing important characters.

The Beirut Banyan Podcast | Lebanon | 2019

Matrix Tower

Matrix Tower is a Mixed-Use building that includes Shopping Center, Restaurants, Residential, Kids Area and Cinema. The Tower is located in Congo, Kinshasa.

Matrix Tower | Congo, Africa | 2017

Haytsh - R Shop

A Retail Shopping store for women clothes. The shop is located in Nabatieh, Lebanon. The Brand Identity was under the title of 'History meets modern art'. The Logo  was inspired from the Phoenician letters 'H' and 'R'. These Phoenician letters were modernized into unique icons that represent the Brand.

Haytsh-R | Nabatieh, Lebanon | 2020
