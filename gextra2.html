La Ween Wasel

'Laween Wasel' is an inclusive public transportation Application that serves 1st Bus Assistant platform in Lebanon. The Application gives its users accurate timing to reach their destination. Moreover, it highlights the need of increase in transportation means for handicap and disability.

My project introduces inclusive public transportation in Lebanon. 'La Ween Wasel' offers an ‘On Time’ navigation system for everyone (inclusive). This is done by providing an application which tracks the bus location by studying traffic (with help of Google maps traffic assumption). 'La Ween Wasel' App will track the exact time needed to arrive at your distination ( taking into consideration the daily traffic). Moreover 'La ween wasel' opened a new door for handicap. This provided for them public transportation means so they can navigate through places easily. 'La Ween Wasel' App introduces ‘Story Telling’ that will make App users interact with different places in Lebanon, sharing the bus ride experience, this helps in creating positive awareness in different cities locally.
